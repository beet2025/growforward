About us

As coaches, we provide support, motivation and feedback to help you achieve your goals.
Whether in everyday life or in business, we believe in the potential of every individual to achive anything they want to. You hold the solutions inside you, and what we do is unlock these solutions to help you achieve your aims and goals..

Danny Argent

Danny has a wealth of experience of coaching over 15 years in his own businesses. Danny is an NLP Master Practitioner and Myers Briggs Practitioner who is passionate about personal and business growth.

Danny has been using NLP and Myers Briggs for around 6 years. His Myers Briggs training was inspired by one of the first people to being the discipline to the UK. He also has a keen interest in Transactional Analysis and Mindfulness.

Danny's skill set ranges from being an excellent personal coach and mentor to an inspiring innovator in business who has driven a variety of SME brands in the north of England.

As a trainer, Danny is very tuned in to individual needs within the group and is a master at drawing on his own experience and examples to bring scenarios to life.

His business experience also extends to being a coach for the Hull University Enterprise Centre for Business.

Chris Colcomb

Chris has been coaching as part of his business for over 15 years. Chris is a Myers Briggs Practitioner and an NLP Master Practitioner.

Chris is also an NLP Trainer from the world-renowned NLP University in Santa Cruz, California. He spent time there absorbing NLP from leaders in the field such as Robert Dilts, Judith Delozier, Suzi Smith and Ian McDermott. Chris is also a member of the Global NLP Training and Consulting Community.

Personal Coaching

A personal or "life" coach helps you to move your life forward and to find fulfilment, success, well-being, and happiness.

We will help you to pinpoint the blocks or obstacles that are making you feel "stuck" and help you to move through them.

We will guide you through a life transition, such as starting a new job, becoming a parent, starting a relationship or entering retirement.

We will help you...

  • Create a positive life plan and make it a reality
  • Develop confidence
  • Manage anxiety
  • Improve relationships
  • Understand others
  • Deal with phobias
  • Deal with stress
  • Achieve happiness

We also offer...

Sessions that explore your personality and give you some fascinating insights about yourself.
